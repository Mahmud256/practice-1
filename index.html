My Travel Blog

Rangamati, the largest District of Bangladesh located amongst the green hills, lots of beautiful lakes and rivers of the Chittagong Hill Tracts. It is the administrative headquarter of the Rangamati Hill District and located near about 77 kilometers far away from Chittagong. I recently visited Rangamati city. It was my first time to Rangamati and I was mesmerized by the beauty and culture of the city. I spent my days at Kaptai Lake and the mountains, hiking in the mountain forests and sampling delicious food.
